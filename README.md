# Skill Half-Life in the Age of AI

## Overview
This project presents a research-grade analytical dashboard examining how
Artificial Intelligence influences the longevity, relevance, and reskilling
requirements of professional skills across industries.

Built for academic panels, AI researchers, and policy evaluators, the dashboard
applies Business Intelligence design principles with research rigor.

## Key Questions Addressed
- Which skills decay fastest under AI exposure?
- How does automation risk interact with market demand?
- Which industries face the highest reskilling pressure?
- What learning strategies support long-term skill resilience?

## Dataset
Synthetic workforce dataset modeling:
- Skill half-life
- AI exposure
- Automation risk
- Market demand
- Reskilling frequency
- Industry and category segmentation
